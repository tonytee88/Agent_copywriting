## CORE LAYOUT (TYPE #1)
1. **HERO BLOCK**: Title + Subtitle + CTA. Must be punchy. No Emojis.
2. **DESCRIPTIVE BLOCK**: Title + Subtitle + Content. This is the "Meat" (Value/Transformation). Ends with `cta_descriptive`.
3. **PRODUCT BLOCK**: Title + Subtitle + List of Products + CTA.

## GLOBAL STYLE RULES (STRICT)
1. **NO INTRO/OUTRO**: Start immediately with the Subject/Hero. Do NOT write "Hi [Name]", "Best regards", or "Cheers".
2. **NO DASHES**: ABSOLUTELY NO hyphens (-), en-dashes (–), or em-dashes (—). Rewrite sentences to avoid them.
3. **BOLDING**: Use `<b>` for *keywords* only (1-3 words). NEVER bold an entire line.
4. **NUMBERS**: Always use digits (5, 65%), never words (five).

## CTA RULES
- **Promotional**: "Shop Now", "Get Yours"
- **Educational**: "View Tools", "See Collection"
- **BANNED**: "Learn More", "Read More"
